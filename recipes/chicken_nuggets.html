Chicken Nuggets

Chicken nuggets are a real kid-pleaser! Adults will enjoy this recipe for a tasty appetizer, too! Serve warm, with your favorite dipping sauces if desired.

Ingredients

  • 3 skinless, boneless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup butter, melted

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Ingredients to make baked chicken nuggets
  2. Cut chicken breasts into 1 1/2-inch pieces. A cutting board with cubed pieces of chicken breast with a knife
  3. Mix the bread crumbs, Parmesan, basil, thyme, and salt together well in a medium bowl. Put melted butter in a bowl or dish for dipping. A bowl of seasoned bread crumbs next to a bowl of melted butter
  4. Dip chicken pieces into the melted butter first, then coat with the bread crumb mixture. A large bowl of cubed chicken breast in seasoned bread crumbs, next to a bowl of cubed chicken breast pieces in melted butter
  5. Place the well-coated chicken nuggets on a lightly greased cookie sheet in a single layer. A baking sheet with bread crumb-coated pieces of chicken breast
  6. Bake nuggets in the preheated oven until browned and chicken is cooked through, about 20 minutes. A baking sheet with baked chicken nuggets
  7. Serve and enjoy! A platter of baked chicken nuggets served with dipping sauce
